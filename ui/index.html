Features

Everything a living knowledge base needs

🔗

Auto cross-linking

Every concept and entity is linked across all documents automatically. 1,400+ links created without any manual tagging.

⚡

Parallel ingest

Upload 50 documents at once. WikiOS processes them in parallel with zero race conditions on shared state.

🔍

Synthesized Q&A

Ask questions in plain language. Get cited answers that draw from across the entire knowledge base.

📊

Gap analysis

WikiOS tells you what's missing — concepts mentioned but not explained, topics with thin coverage, documents to add next.

🏢

Vertical schemas

Pre-built schemas for legal, pharma, and finance. Or define your own page types, fields, and depth standards.

🔒

Self-hosted

Bring your own Anthropic API key and run WikiOS entirely on your infrastructure. Your documents never leave.
